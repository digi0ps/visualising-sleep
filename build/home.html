Sleep Visualisation

Visualising the sleep cycle and food of college students to analyse the correlation between their sleep and food pattern

How we collected the dataset

We collected data of four students of diverse backgrounds from VIT university. The data we got from them included their last eaten food, how much hours they slept, how good they felt the next morning.

The preprocessing

Since all the data was entered by the users, they weren't proper in natue and had lot of unnecessary details. We had to clean the data by changing the slept time to the right hour time, having the right format for names, dates and food category.

Visualisation and Analysis

With all the preprocessed data, we set on our paths to create the perfect visualisation for that. Visualisation is a great tool to create awareness of the bad sleep cycle of students. So keeping that in mind we made beautiful visualisations.
